About

My name is Claire Wanjiru. I am an aspiring software engineer who is passionate about this field. My motivation is the eagerness to build products that solve everyday problems and make it efficient for people while securing them and their personal data from vulnerability to cybercrimes. I’m working to achieve this in order to be an inspiration to the youth, especially ladies who think of venturing into the field of IT but are held back by a certain mindset like, "It is a male-dominated industry and as a lady, you’ll struggle too much...". Ladies should remember that "What a man can do, a woman can do better!". Besides that, I would like everyone to believe in themselves and believe that they can be who they’ve always dreamed of as long as they work towards their goal determinedly and with a lot of focus.
